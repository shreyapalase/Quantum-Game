⚛ Quantum Uncertainty War

AAA Sci-Fi Quantum Simulation Arena

Game Introduction

You are inside a quantum battlefield where particles exist in superposition. Your mission: collapse quantum states strategically and win probabilistic war rounds.

How to Play

  • Click “Generate Quantum State”
  • System creates superposition |0⟩ + |1⟩
  • Measurement collapses probability
  • Higher probability wins the round

Qiskit Reference Circuit

from qiskit import QuantumCircuit

qc = QuantumCircuit(1,1)
qc.h(0)          # superposition
qc.measure(0,0)  # collapse
