⚛ QUANTUM BLOCH SPHERE ⚛

Ultra Hyper Realistic Quantum State Simulator

Game Description

A Bloch Sphere is a visual representation of a qubit. Apply quantum gates and rotate the quantum state vector. Measure the qubit and see whether you reach the target state.

How To Play

  • Start from |0⟩ state.
  • Apply quantum gates.
  • Observe state vector movement.
  • Measure the qubit.
  • Reach |1⟩ to win.

Win / Loss Conditions

WIN → Collapse to |1⟩
LOSS → Collapse to |0⟩
