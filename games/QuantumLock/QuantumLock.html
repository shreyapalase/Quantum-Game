QUANTUM LOCK

Ultra Realistic Quantum Computing Learning Simulator

Game Introduction

A futuristic security vault is protected using quantum states. Your objective is to discover the hidden quantum key before your measurement budget expires.

User Guide

  • Initialize Qubits
  • Apply Quantum Gates
  • Observe Probability Distribution
  • Measure Quantum Lock
  • Find Correct Quantum State

Win Condition

Reach probability > 80% for hidden target state and measure correctly.

Loss Condition

Measurements exhausted or probability collapses incorrectly.

Pauli-X acts like a quantum NOT gate.
