⚡ Superposition Game

Qubit State: |ψ⟩ = α|0⟩ + β|1⟩

|0⟩: 50% | |1⟩: 50%

from qiskit import QuantumCircuit
qc = QuantumCircuit(1,1)
qc.h(0)
qc.measure(0,0)

🎮 How to Play

1. Rotate qubit to change probability

2. Measure to collapse state

3. Try to get |1⟩ to win

🏆 Win Condition

Measurement = |1⟩

💀 Lose Condition

Measurement = |0⟩
