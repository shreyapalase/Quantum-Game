⚛ Quantum Maze Runner

Navigate a maze that exists in quantum superposition

🎓 How To Play

  • The maze is not fixed — it exists in multiple states.
  • You choose quantum operations to bias probability.
  • Press “Run Quantum Step” to evolve system.
  • Measurement collapses maze into one final reality.
  • Reach exit node to win.

Controls

  • Z Gate → stabilizes current path
  • X Gate → flips probability direction
  • H Gate → creates superposition (multi-path)

🧠 Quantum Concept Used

This game simulates a Quantum Random Walk where a particle explores multiple maze paths simultaneously until measurement collapses it.

💻 Quantum Circuit (Qiskit)


from qiskit import QuantumCircuit

qc = QuantumCircuit(3)

qc.h([0,1,2])   # superposition paths

qc.cx(0,1)

qc.cx(1,2)

qc.measure_all()
